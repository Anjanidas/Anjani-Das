    INGRDIENTS:

  • Utensils(pan $ mixer)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ginger(crushed)
  • Cardamom(crushed)
  • Tea(powder)

    INSTUCTIONS:

  1. Put the milk in the pan and let it boil.
  2. Once the milk is boiling properly,put the tea powder and sugarin the pan and let it boil for a while.
  3. After a while, add the crushed Ginger and the Cardamom into the boiling milk along with the tea.
  4. Mix it for a while with all the ingredients untill the tea becomes reddish brown.
